    Neovasc posts loss of $29.1 million in third quarter

    RICHMOND, British Columbia (AP) _ Neovasc Inc. (NVCN) on Monday reported a loss of $29.1 million in its third quarter.

    On a per-share basis, the Richmond, British Columbia-based company said it had a loss of 44 cents. Losses, adjusted for non-recurring costs, were 12 cents per share.

    The medical device company posted revenue of $3 million in the period.

    In the final minutes of trading on Monday, the company's shares hit 58 cents. A year ago, they were trading at $4.27.

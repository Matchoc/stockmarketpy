    CPI Card Group posts 3Q profit, results miss Street expectations

    Associated Press – Thu, Nov 10, 2016 6:57 AM SGT

    Companies:

    • CPI Card Group Inc.

    RELATED QUOTES

    SymbolPriceChange
    PMTS3.85+0.20

    LITTLETON, Colo. (AP) _ CPI Card Group Inc. (PMTS) on Wednesday reported third-quarter net income of $4 million.

    The Littleton, Colorado-based company said it had profit of 7 cents per share. Earnings, adjusted for one-time gains and costs, came to 11 cents per share.

    The results did not meet Wall Street expectations. The average estimate of three analysts surveyed by Zacks Investment Research was for earnings of 15 cents per share.

    The payment cards company posted revenue of $81.2 million in the period, which also did not meet Street forecasts. Four analysts surveyed by Zacks expected $84.4 million.

    For the current quarter ending in December, CPI Card expects its per-share earnings to range from 24 cents to 26 cents.

    CPI Card shares have declined 53 percent since the beginning of the year. In the final minutes of trading on Wednesday, shares hit $5.05, a fall of 57 percent in the last 12 months.
